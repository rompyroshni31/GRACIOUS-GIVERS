Street Food Box

An innovative initiative to save food for the ones who reaally need it.

OUR IDEA
(Hover on me)

We have seen many people craving for food and dying on the streets. So we felt like we should help them with our technology and devices because why not. In our personal lives, many of the times we're stuck in a situation where we have leftover food items and you have to throw is unnescesarily. So we tried to take a step to solve this. Basically our idea is that we want to install a system over some possible public places. A system in which there's a refrigerator fitted to store the food. You can navigate the nearest systems/boxes present through this site and can put your leftover food into it. Our members will take out that food from the respective systems and give the edible checked food to the people who're dying of hunger.
